Parliamentary Bill Tracking

Try to track the history of a particular bill in parliament. Do it, we dare you. It's not very easy. The process of making laws is difficult to follow. In South Africa, the workflow is extremely confusing. It's difficult to track a bill's history, how it changed and more importantly, the discussions around the change. Only very dedicated researchers are able to piece this puzzle together.

What We Did

Code4SA is trying to change all that. Working with the Parliamentary Monitoring Group (PMG) we are collating all the relevant information in one place. Latest version of the bill? No problem. Audio recordings of discussions by parliamentary committees? Here they are. Changes in the text from one version to another ... we're working on that.

In principle the information is all there ... a microsecond before they remove it from the website, or locked up in pdf documents labeled useful names such as 'mn31e-13-20june'. Using the hardwork of PMG along with some clever scraping trickery from Code4SA, we are creating a little corner of the internet where you can cosy up to you favourite piece of legislation and walk down memory lane remembering your favourite clauses that have since been dropped.

Oh the good old days!
