Tools for Journos

Code4SA's curated list of tools for journalists for writing, finding and investigating stories.

Wazimap.co.za

Detailed demographics and election results for everywhere in South Africa, right down to the ward level. Age, income, household goods, employment, toilets, water and other service delivery details across the country from the 2011 Census.

OpenByLaws.org.za

Full local by-laws for Cape Town and Johannesburg, with more on the way. Easy to search, read and share.

People's Assembly - pa.org.za

Biographies, contact details and declared insterests of all South African Members of Parliament.

Parliamentary Monitoring Group - PMG.org.za

Everything from the day-to-day workings of parliament, including committee memberships, meeting reports, hansards and questions and replies.

SA Newsmakers

Who shows up the most in South African media and why?
