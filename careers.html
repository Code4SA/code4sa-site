Data Wrangler

Contract position for a data wrangler to liberate data for social good. The role will involve working closely with a range of civic organisations to identify interesting data sources that can be cleaned and prepared for consumption as open data. You will also be writing weekly blog posts and presenting interesting stories you have found using infographics and other visualisations.

Requirements

  • Self-confessed data junkie
  • Hands-on experience with data processing
  • Proficient using data management or statistical tools (e.g. Excel, R, Stata, etc)
  • Able to explore datasets and identify interesting anecdotes and stories
  • Able to develop data visualisations
  • Writing skills
  • Member of the open data community (preferred)
  • Able to work independently

Start Date: August / September 2015
Salary: In line with experience and market related
Location: Cape Town

To apply, contact jobs@code4sa.org.

Developer

Code for SA is a civic technology lab. Everything we do is open source and often draws public attention. You’ll get to work on a wide variety of projects which keeps it interesting and means there’s lots to learn. We’re a small team so you can get your hands dirty in almost everything we do and there are opportunities to travel to talk about what we do both locally and internationally.

We're based in Codebridge in beautiful Cape Town and have flexible working conditions.

We use industry best practices and technologies, including:

  • Python, Django and Flask
  • GitHub - github.com/Code4SA
  • Heroku, Amazon EC2, S3 and RDS
  • Agile processes using Trello and Pivotal Tracker
  • Bootstrap, D3, Leaflet

You should be familiar with the technology stack we use (or keen to learn) and have 2-3 years experience under your belt.

Most of all, you should want to make a difference to your city and South Africa. Come and join us!

Send an email to jobs@code4sa.org if you're keen to make an impact.

Technical Business Analyst/Project Manager

Do you love figuring stuff out and helping companies unlock their potential? Do you love actively making a difference in your community and would jump at the chance to make a difference through your job? Why not speak to us? We have a contract position for a Technical BA/PM to unlock the potential within our partnered civic organisations, for social good.

We are looking for an independent, switched-on technical PM/BA to join our growing team, someone who grab a project by the reins and run with it. An all-rounder, the ideal candidate will be comfortable eliciting requirements from client partners as well as facilitate solutioning and implementation. Experience in business analysis and project management is essential along with a healthy appetite for data. Technical experience would be the absolute cherry on top.

This role has scope to grow with the organisation and we would prefer to place someone who enjoys the idea of growing with us.

Role and Responsibilities

  • Requirements elicitation – teasing out the actual requirements from clients, not just what they think they need.
  • Data analysis – gathering and analysing data from across the organisation.
  • Spec creation – reporting on existing situation, current problems or opportunities and proposed solutions. Knowledge of various diagramming formats required.
  • Project coordination – ensuring time and budget is handled correctly.
  • Data management strategy – facilitating the overall strategy required in the organisation to assist them in realising their overall vision.
  • Client management – ensuring the client is constantly updated of the state of the project.

Requirements

  • Passion for civic technology and data
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal
  • Analytical thinking
  • Comfortable with the SDLC and agile methodologies
  • Able to iterate through the requirements, in phases
  • Excellent writing skills
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to work independently
  • B.Com IS or Dip. BA advantageous

Start Date: August / September 2015
Salary: In line with experience and market related
Location: Cape Town

To apply, contact jobs@code4sa.org.

About Code for South Africa

Code for South Africa is a non-profit civic technology lab. We use data and technology to promote informed decision-making for social good. Our base of operations is Codebridge in Claremont where we are helping to create a civic technology movement.
